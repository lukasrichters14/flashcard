Instructions

1. Copy and paste your notes into a text file. The file should have a .txt extension, not .doc, .docx, etc.

2. Click on the "Choose File" button on the main page.

3. After you have uploaded your file (you will know because your file name will appear to the right of the "Choose File" button) click on the "Upload" button.

4. Your formatted flashcard text should appear below the "Here is your fully-formatted flashcard set" text. Be sure to copy it!

5. Navigate to Quizlet.com and log in (if necessary).

6. Click on the "Create" button.

7. Just below the title of your new set will be a "Import from..." link. Click on it.

8. Paste your formatted text into the large box at the top of the screen.

9. There are two fields for you to fill out just below the large box. In the first one ("Between term and definition"), enter the "@" character. In the second one ("Between cards"), enter the "~!" characters.

10. Click the "Import" button, and you will now have your own Quizlet study set.

I hope this guide was informative a helpful, and that you ace whatever quiz or test you are preparing for.
